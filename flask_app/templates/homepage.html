Graduating in Life

Inspired by news from the BBC "The Happy Podcast", our focus is to promote opportunities for the senior members of our communities to celebrate life. Here administrators of senior living communities and/or other inspired members of our society can create events that resemble a high school senior prom for those who could have actually graduated twice over.

Seniors attending are encouraged to bring photos or other small items to share from the days they were in school.

If you would like to see the events we have available or you felt inspired and want to promote an event, please Login or Register.
